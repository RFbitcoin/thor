A score of 78% means the technical, macro, on-chain, and sentiment signals are all pointing the same way simultaneously. That's a different class of signal entirely.

Platform Features

A full analytical workstation — not another indicator dashboard.

🧠

ML-Adaptive Pillar Weights

Pillar weights are recalculated via logistic regression on each backtest window. THOR adapts to changing market regimes automatically.

🔬

Walk-Forward Backtesting

Trains on one data window, evaluates on the next unseen window. The institutional standard for preventing overfitting — rare in retail tools.

📰

NLP News Sentiment

Local VADER sentiment scoring on live headlines from CoinDesk, Cointelegraph, Bitcoin Magazine, and Reuters. Integrated into the 24h prediction model.

📊

Your Hardware. Your Keys. Your Data.

THOR runs entirely on a Raspberry Pi 5 you own. No cloud subscription, no third-party custody of your API keys, no exchange credentials leaving your local network.

After FTX and Celsius, self-custody isn't a niche preference — it's the obvious choice. THOR's architecture was built from the ground up around this principle.

The platform is served via Cloudflare Tunnel so you can access it anywhere, but the data, the keys, and the signal engine stay on your own hardware at all times.
